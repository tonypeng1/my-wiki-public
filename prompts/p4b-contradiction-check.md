Perform a targeted contradiction check on the wiki.
Run this occasionally — it is not part of the standard health check.

1. Read all files in wiki/concepts/.

2. CONTRADICTIONS
   Focus first within each clinical domain (concepts sharing a tag),
   since cross-domain contradictions are rare. Look for claims across
   concept articles that directly contradict each other — especially
   specific values, dates, reference ranges, and test results. List
   any you find with the specific conflicting statements and the files
   they appear in. Do not resolve them automatically — flag them for
   my review.

3. Report your findings. If no contradictions are found,
   say so explicitly so I know the check ran cleanly.
